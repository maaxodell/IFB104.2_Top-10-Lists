The Weekly Top 10 Non-Fiction Bestseller List

At number 1 this week is The Barefoot Investor for Families – aimed fairly and squarely at parents, grandparents, and basically anyone who read The Barefoot Investor and said: ‘Why the hell wasn’t I taught this years ago?’

At number 2 this week is the 2018 updated copy of The Barefoot Investor by Scott Pape.

At number 3 this week is Any Ordinary Day by Leigh Sales – a warm, candid and empathetic book about what happens when ordinary people, on ordinary days, are forced to suddenly find the resilience most of us don’t know we have.

At number 4 is the combined edition of The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*** by Mark Manson, a no-nonsense guide on how to focus on the things that really matter.

At number 5 we have Simple by Yotam Ottolenghi, with 130 brand-new dishes that contain all the inventive elements and flavour combinations that Ottolenghi is loved for, but with minimal hassle for maximum joy.

At number 6 is The CSIRO Healthy Gut Guide. Written by a team of experienced CSIRO researchers, including nutritional scientists and dieticians, many of whom are internationally recognised authorities in nutrition and gut health, it contains simple, practical advice and a wide range of tasty, easy-to-make recipes designed to benefit the gut and overall health.

At number 7 is I Quit Sugar – your guide to kicking the habit, losing weight and getting well.

At number 8 is Boys Will be Boys – the incendiary new book about toxic masculinity and misogyny from Clementine Ford, author of the best-selling feminist manifesto, Fight Like A Girl.

The world’s most popular record book is back with thousands of new categories and newly broken records, covering everything from outer space to sporting greats via Instagram, fidget spinners and all manner of human marvels. Guinness World Records 2019 lands at number 9 this week.

At number 10 we have Challenge Accepted! by Celeste Barber –  a hilarious and outspoken guide to life, unwanted gas and how to rock a sexy scar. It’s real, like totally, really real.

Check out our Top Ten Fiction List and Top Ten Children’s List. That’s it from us this week at Better Reading. Happy Reading!

  1. The Barefoot Investor for Families by Scott Pape (HarperCollins)
  2. The Barefoot Investor by Scott Pape (Giramondo Publishing)
  3. Any Ordinary Day by Leigh Sales (Hamish Hamilton)
  4. The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*** by Mark Manson (Macmillan)
  5. Ottolenghi Simple by Yotam Ottolenghi (Ebury Press)
  6. The CSIRO Healthy Gut Diet by Pennie Taylor, Michael Conlon, and Tony Bird (Macmillan)
  7. I Quit Sugar by Sarah Wilson (Macmillan)
  8. Boys Will be Boys by Clementine Ford (Allen & Unwin)
  9. Guinness World Records 2019 by Guinness World Records (Guinness World Records Limited)
  10. Challenge Accepted! by Celeste Barber (HarperCollins)
